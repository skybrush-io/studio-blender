= Drone Groups Panel
:imagesdir: ../../../assets/images
:experimental:

The Drone Groups panel lets you set up simple drone groups for later use with formations, light effects and for other general purposes.

image::panels/drone_groups/drone_groups.jpg[Drone Groups panel]

== Drone collection

Drone groups consist of your drone objects separated into drone group collections.

Click on the btn:[Drone group collection] dropdown to select the collection that is your primary source of your drone group lists. By default you do not have to change this setting, the btn:[Create Takeoff Grid] button below will create a "Drone Groups" collection and set it as your primary source automatically.

IMPORTANT: Any sub-collection placed in the collection selected here will be treated as a drone group by *Skybrush Studio*. Make sure that there are no other objects in these groups than the ones that you want to consider drones.

=== Creating, renaming and deleting drone groups

Press the btn:[+] button on the right of the panel to add a new drone group to the list of groups. Double click on it to set a custom name for your new group. Press the btn:[-] button to remove the selected group from the list.

Alternatively, you can use Blender's standard tools in the Outliner editor to modify your Drone Groups collections and its contents.

=== Operations with drone groups

The list of drone groups shows you the names and the number of drones in all groups. Select one group from the list to perform operations with it.

=== Add 

Press the btn:[Add] button on the bottom of the panel to add the currently selected drones to the given drone group.

=== Select

Press the btn:[Select] button on the bottom of the panel to select the drones from the currently selected drone group.

=== Clear

Press the btn:[Clear] button on the bottom of the panel to remove all drones from the selected drone group.
